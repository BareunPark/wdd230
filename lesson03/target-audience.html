Target Audience

Local residents, visitors, and businesses in Seoul

Personas


Name Age Job
Sun-woo 23 Student

Sun-woo is a local resident interested in finding new businesses and events in the city.


Name Age Job
Jung-ho 50 A business owner

Jung-ho is a business owner looking for resources and networking opportunities.

Scenarios for Persona 1


  • Sun-woo is planning a date night with her partner and wants to find a new restaurant to try in the city. She visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to browse a list of recommended local restaurants.
  • Sun-woo is looking for a new yoga studio to join. She visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to find a list of local gyms and fitness centers, and their class schedules.
  • Sun-woo's parents are coming to visit and she wants to find a unique tourist attraction to take them to. She visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to browse a list of popular tourist attractions in the city.
  • Sun-woo needs to buy a gift for a friend's birthday and wants to support a local business. She visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to find a list of local boutiques and gift shops.
  • Sun-woo is a freelancer looking for networking opportunities in the city. She visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to find information about upcoming business events and workshops.

Scenarios for Persona 2


  • Jung-ho is looking to hire a new employee and wants to post a job listing on the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to attract local talent.
  • Jung-ho wants to learn more about business regulations and requirements in Seoul. He visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to access resources and information about city regulations and permits.
  • Jung-ho is a new business owner and needs advice on how to attract more customers. He visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to find information about marketing resources and strategies.
  • Jung-ho wants to connect with other business owners in the city. He visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to find information about upcoming networking events.
  • Jung-ho wants to expand his business and is looking for a new commercial space. He visits the Seoul Chamber of Commerce website to browse a list of available commercial properties in the city.
